process {{< partial "svg/arrow-right-down.svg" >}}

01 Discovery

From your idea to complete product tool kit. We go over what you have and don’t have, together. We establish goals, together. And we move forward only when both sides are confident and fully understood.

Outputs (market research, user personas, product specifications, rough cost & time estimate)

02 Design

Thanks to the previous phase, the product has a strong identity. Thanks to this, we know what we are creating and we start working on how we create. We involve you in the whole process. We consult with you on an ongoing basis about the solution and its impact on cost and time.

Outputs (UX/UI, developer guidance, reevaluation of cost & time estimate)

03 Engineering

After approval of the design, we begin with developing it. We continuously test the product and regularly inform you about the development status so so you always have a clear insight into the progress.

Outputs (combining previous phases & engineering the product)

04 Maintanance & scaling

Our work does not end with the release. Every digital product, app or website requires ongoing care. Your product can become that the best, but stagnation is bad for it. By continuous improvements we can ensure that it stays there.

Outputs(analytics, post-release product insights, KPI tracking, marketing, social media management, product improvements)
